## What is Synera?

Synera is a powerful, seamless integrated Discord-Roblox ranking bot that bridges the gap between your Discord community and Roblox group management. With Synera, you can easily manage your Roblox group rankings directly from Discord, automate member promotions, and create a unified experience for your community.

## Getting Started

Ready to transform your Discord-Roblox community management? Here's how to get started:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Invite Synera">
    Add Synera to your Discord server using our [invitation link](https://discord.com/oauth2/authorize?client_id=YOUR_BOT_ID).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run Setup">
    Use the `/setup_community` command to configure your Roblox group integration.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Permissions">
    Set up roles and permissions to control who can use ranking commands.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start Ranking">
    Begin using Synera's powerful ranking commands to manage your community.
  </Step>
</Steps>
